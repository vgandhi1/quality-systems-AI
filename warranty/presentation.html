The problem

Claims bundles are messy.
Adjusters are the bottleneck.

  • 4–7 days per claim — forms, OBD-II scans, labor hours, and policy cross-checks.
  • Policy drift — coverage depends on VIN year, component, and required attachments.
  • Fraud signals — labor variance vs OEM matrix and dealership velocity patterns.
  • Async waits — missing voltage logs or photos pause the workflow for days.
Synthetic fixtures only in this POC — no real customer VINs, claim records, or payment data.

Routing

Three outcomes. One authoritative gate.

AUTO_APPROVE

Covered · fraud < 30 · cost < $1,500

MISSING_DATA

Policy requires docs not in bundle

HUMAN_REVIEW

Fraud ≥ 70 · high cost · edge cases

  • LLM submit_decision is advisory — route_claim() in validation.py is authoritative.
  • Pinned thresholds re-checked by evaluate.py --check on every CI run.
